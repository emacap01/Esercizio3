La Boca

Studio

La Boca’s studio, founded in 2002, is based in west London and has gathered an envious collection of awards, including Cannes Lions, European Design Awards, and the AOI Illustration Awards.

Portfolio

Their psychedelic style is the result of each of the studio's members' influences.

La Boca’s Teamwork

“We work as a studio, so most of our designs are created as a team,” says Scott Bendall of La Boca. “It's not unusual to have two or three people working on a single piece.”
