Karlotta Freier

Illustrator

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist. In 2021, she graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts.

Portfolio

Freier’s work has been exposed worldwide, in solo or group exhibitions.

Freier’s accomplishment

Her achievements have been recognized by the Art Directors Club, which awarded her a Cube in 2018 and a Pencil for Best Illustration Portfolio in 2021.
