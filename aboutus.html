About us.

ArtAgency has been created in 2011 from Camilo Huinca and Tim Lahan after long years of friendship and teamwork. The group later expanded, becoming what it is today.

Our work does make sense only if it is a faithful witness of his time.

Xoana Herrera, Illustrator

The team.

ArtAgency is a group of illustrators, animators and graphic designers from all around the globe.

Meet Camilo Huinca, Tim Lahan, Karotta Feier, Xoana Herrera, Linn Fritz and the studio La Boca.
