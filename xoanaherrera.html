Xoana Herrera

Graphic designer, illustrator

Xoana is a LA - based illustrator, trained as a graphic designer in Argentina but worked as illustrator at Buck Agency.

Portfolio

Herrera’s portfolio is an explosion of stylish caracters in an ideal world.

“As artists we must keep evolving”

Alongside founding a feminist creative collective, Get it Girl, she creates graceful, complex and accessible imagery which would be as at home in an ad campaign as it would be in a children’s book.
