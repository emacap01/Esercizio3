Tim Lahan

Illustrator

Tim Lahan is a San-Francisco’s based artist. He’s an ADC Young Gun award winner and his style is easily recognizable.

Portfolio

Tim’s style is simple and funny

Tim Lahan’s artwork is the product of his ability to digest and illustrate clever glitches, concepts and interesting visual cues from the world around him.
